Ideal Customer Profile & Buyer Map

Focus: Nordic, mid‑market organisations with distributed HR data and strategic transformation agendas.

ICP

  • 1,000–5,000 employees, Nordic HQ
  • Multi‑system HR stack (e.g., HRIS + ATS + LMS)
  • Microsoft 365 / Power BI ecosystem
  • Ambition to elevate HR as a strategic partner

Primary Persona

HR Director (Primary)

Accountable for people strategy & reporting to executive/board. Seeks faster decisions, credibility, and enablement for HRBPs.

CHRO

Sponsor for strategic roadmap and governance; ensures alignment with business outcomes.

IT / Data

Gatekeeper for security and deployment; prefers in‑tenant solutions and clear operational ownership.
