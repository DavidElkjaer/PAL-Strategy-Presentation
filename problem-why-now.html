The Problem We Solve

Mid‑sized organisations face an analytics capability gap—and rising regulatory expectations.

Capability Gap
  • Too small to justify 2–3 FTE analytics hires
  • HR data scattered across multiple systems
  • Manual reporting consumes scarce HR time
  • DIY tools introduce hidden cost and inconsistency
Why Now
  • CSRD & EU Pay Transparency push board‑level reporting
  • Demand for predictive and scenario insight, not just dashboards
  • Expectation for faster time‑to‑value and ongoing enablement
  • Need for solutions that run inside the company’s own tenant
