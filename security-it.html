Security & IT Assurance

Built for IT and data governance from day one.

Principles
  • All data, models and reports run in the client’s tenant
  • Role‑based access & auditability
  • GDPR compliant processes and data minimisation
  • Source‑of‑truth integration & versioned dataflows
Environment Requirements
  • Microsoft 365 and Power BI (Pro or Premium per capacity)
  • Secure connectors for HRIS/ATS/LMS (or flat‑file landing)
  • Designated data owner and service account
  • Access to SharePoint/OneDrive for controlled extracts
