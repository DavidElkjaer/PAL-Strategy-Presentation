Market Opportunity

€75M TAM in the Nordic mid-market
The Analytics Gap in Mid-Market Companies

Mid-sized European companies (1,000–5,000 employees) often collect extensive HR data but lack the in-house capacity to convert it into decisions.

Current Challenges

  • Too small to justify 2–3 FTE analytics hires (€200K+ annually)
  • Fragmented systems across HRIS, payroll, ATS
  • Compliance pressure with growing regulatory requirements

Market Dynamics Driving Urgency

  • CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)
  • EU pay transparency directive
  • Talent scarcity requiring better workforce planning
  • Widespread HRIS adoption—more mid-sized firms now have structured workforce data ready for analytics
